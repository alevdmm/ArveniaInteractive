Page Release Details



The launch of this website has brought new and convenient features to help you explore our videogames. The following features were added in version 1.0:

- News Page: The news section will feature the latest news within the community, including news from Discord, Roblox, and the website itself.

- Community Page: The community page will contain direct links to each group, including the Roblox and Discord groups.

- Games Page: The games section will showcase completed and currently under development games, along with their versions and a "Play" button. Each game has its own individual page; clicking the "Play" button will display a description, image, title, and a direct link to the game.

- Team Page In the team section, you will find all contributors and developers listed by name. This section is where all developers will be credited, and the story of the birth of the Arvenia Interactive community will be told.
